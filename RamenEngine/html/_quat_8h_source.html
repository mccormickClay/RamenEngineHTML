18  /*************************************************************************
19  *
20  * This class contains functions and data structure for a 4-dimensional
21  * Quaternions. The quaternions are internally stored interms of the
22  * imaginary components (x,y,z) and the real components (real).
23  *
24  * In the Math library, quaternions can be thought of as "rotational"
25  * matrices, everything that you can represent with rotational matrices
26  * you can use with quaternions. Such as quaternion concatenation,
27  * inverses, transpose, normalize, multiplying with matrices, and with
28  * vectors.
29  *
30  *
31  **************************************************************************/
